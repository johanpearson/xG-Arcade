Signature interaction

Badge dock

On reveal, the row's flag/badge and the column's badge slide in from either side and settle beside the player name — a literal visualization of "these two categories combined into this answer," tied directly to the actual mechanic rather than a borrowed broadcast trope. Loops here for demo purposes only; in the real product it plays once. Reduced-motion: badges appear already docked with a brief color flash instead of sliding.
